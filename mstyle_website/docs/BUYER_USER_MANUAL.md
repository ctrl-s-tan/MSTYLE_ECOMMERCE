## Getting Started

### Welcome to M'STYLE

M'STYLE is your premier online destination for men's fashion and grooming products. Shop from verified sellers, enjoy secure payments, and get fast delivery right to your doorstep.

### Creating an Account

#### Registration Process
1. Click "Register" on the homepage
2. Fill in required information:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
   - Confirm Password
3. Read and accept Terms & Conditions
4. Click "Create Account"
5. Verify your email address
6. Complete your profile

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character

#### Email Verification
1. Check your email inbox
2. Open verification email from M'STYLE
3. Click verification link
4. Account activated
5. Start shopping!

### Logging In

#### Sign In Process
1. Click "Login" on homepage
2. Enter email address
3. Enter password
4. Click "Sign In"
5. Access your account

#### Forgot Password
1. Click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click reset link
5. Create new password
6. Confirm new password
7. Login with new password

### First-Time Setup

#### Complete Your Profile
1. Add profile picture
2. Add delivery address
3. Add phone number
4. Set notification preferences
5. Add payment methods (optional)

#### Explore the Platform
- Browse categories
- View featured products
- Check promotions
- Add items to wishlist
- Start shopping!

## Profile Management

### Overview
Manage your seller profile, business information, and account settings.

### Accessing Your Profile
1. Click on your profile icon in the top navigation bar
2. Select "Profile" from the dropdown menu
3. Your profile page will display with your current information

### Profile Information Sections

#### 1. Personal Information
**Editable Fields:**
- First Name
- Last Name
- Email Address
- Phone Number
- Business Name
- Address
- Profile Picture

**How to Update:**
1. Click the "Edit Profile" button
2. All fields become editable (highlighted with a border)
3. Modify the desired fields
4. Click "Save Changes" button
5. Success notification appears: "Profile updated successfully!"

**Profile Picture Upload:**
1. Click on your profile avatar/picture
2. Select an image file from your device
3. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP
4. Maximum file size: 5MB
5. Image uploads automatically and updates your profile

#### 2. Change Password Section
**Always Visible Below Profile Form:**

**Required Fields:**
- Current Password
- New Password
- Confirm New Password

**How to Change Password:**
1. Scroll to the "Change Password" section
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click "Update Password" button
6. Success message appears and form clears

**Password Requirements:**
- Must match your current password for verification
- New password must match confirmation
- All fields are required

### Form Validation
- All required fields must be filled before saving
- Email must be in valid format
- Phone number should be valid
- Success/error notifications appear at the top of the page

### Best Practices
- Keep contact information up to date
- Use a professional profile picture
- Use a strong password and change it regularly
- Ensure your business name is accurate for customer trust
- Keep your address current for accurate delivery coordination

## Seller Dashboard

### Overview
Your central hub for monitoring business performance, orders, and key metrics. The dashboard provides real-time insights into your store's performance.

### Accessing the Dashboard
1. Log in to your seller account
2. You'll automatically land on the Seller Dashboard
3. Or click "Dashboard" in the navigation menu

### Dashboard Sections

#### 1. Key Metrics Cards (Top Row)
Five main statistics cards display your business performance:

**Total Sales**
- Shows total revenue from all completed orders
- Displayed in Philippine Peso (₱)
- Includes all successful transactions

**Total Earnings**
- Your net earnings after platform commission (5%)
- Actual amount you receive
- Excludes fees and discounts

**Items Sold**
- Total number of items sold
- Counts individual product units
- From all completed orders

**Average Order Value**
- Average amount per order
- Calculated from completed orders
- Helps track customer spending patterns

**Total Products**
- Number of products in your inventory
- Includes active products only
- Quick link to product management

#### 2. Sales Chart (Last 12 Months)
**Two Interactive Line Charts:**

**Total Sales Chart:**
- Blue line graph showing monthly sales
- Last 12 months of data
- Hover over points to see exact amounts
- Smooth curved lines for better visualization
- Y-axis shows peso amounts
- X-axis shows months (e.g., "Jan 2025", "Feb 2025")

**Net Earnings Chart:**
- Green line graph showing monthly earnings
- After platform commission deduction
- Same 12-month period
- Interactive hover tooltips
- Shows your actual take-home income

**Chart Features:**
- Responsive design (adapts to screen size)
- Smooth animations
- Clear month labels
- Peso currency formatting
- Visual trend analysis

#### 3. Order Status Distribution (Pie Chart)
**Doughnut Chart Showing:**
- Pending orders (Orange)
- Confirmed orders (Blue)
- Preparing orders (Purple)
- Ready for Pickup (Teal)
- Out for Delivery (Indigo)
- Delivered orders (Light Green)
- Completed orders (Dark Green)
- Cancelled orders (Red)

**Chart Features:**
- Color-coded segments
- Percentage breakdown on hover
- Order count for each status
- Legend at the bottom
- Interactive tooltips showing exact numbers

### Understanding Your Dashboard

#### What the Metrics Mean
- **Total Sales**: Gross revenue before any deductions
- **Total Earnings**: What you actually receive (Sales - 5% commission)
- **Items Sold**: Individual product units, not orders
- **Avg Order Value**: Total sales ÷ number of orders
- **Total Products**: Your active product catalog size

#### Reading the Charts
- **Upward trends**: Growing sales/earnings
- **Downward trends**: Declining performance
- **Flat lines**: Stable business
- **Spikes**: Successful promotions or seasonal peaks

#### Order Status Meanings
- **Pending**: New orders awaiting your confirmation
- **Confirmed**: Orders you've accepted, waiting for rider
- **Preparing**: Orders being prepared for pickup
- **Ready for Pickup**: Packed and ready for rider collection
- **Out for Delivery**: Rider is delivering to customer
- **Delivered**: Customer received the order
- **Completed**: Customer confirmed receipt, payment released
- **Cancelled**: Order was cancelled by you or customer

### Dashboard Actions

#### Quick Navigation
- Click on any metric card to view detailed information
- Access product management directly
- View order lists with one click
- Navigate to reports for deeper analysis

### Best Practices
- Check dashboard daily to monitor performance
- Review sales trends weekly to identify patterns
- Monitor order status distribution for bottlenecks
- Compare month-to-month performance
- Use insights to plan inventory and promotions
- Track earnings to manage cash flow

### Tips for Success
- Aim for high completion rates (green segment)
- Minimize cancellations (red segment)
- Process pending orders quickly
- Monitor seasonal trends in sales charts
- Use data to optimize product offerings

## Add New Product

### Overview
List new products on the platform with detailed information and images. The Add New Product page provides a comprehensive form to create your product listings.

### Accessing Add New Product
1. Click "Products" in navigation menu
2. Click "Add New Product" button (green button, top right)
3. Or navigate directly from dashboard

### Product Form Sections

#### 1. Product Name (Required)
- Enter clear, descriptive product title
- Include brand, style, or key features
- Example: "Men's Classic Fit Cotton Polo Shirt - Navy Blue"
- No character limit, but keep it concise and searchable

#### 2. Description (Required)
- Detailed product description
- Include:
  - Product features
  - Materials/fabric
  - Fit and sizing information
  - Care instructions
  - Benefits and use cases
- Use clear, engaging language
- Minimum 50 characters recommended

#### 3. Category (Required)
**Select from dropdown:**
- 🤵 Suits
- 🧥 Blazers
- 👔 Shirts
- 👖 Pants
- 🧥 Outerwear
- 🧥 Jackets
- 🏃 Activewear
- 💪 Fitness
- 👟 Shoes
- 👜 Accessories
- 🧴 Grooming Products

**Choosing the Right Category:**
- Select the most specific category
- Helps buyers find your product
- Affects search and filtering
- Cannot be changed easily after creation

#### 4. Available Sizes (Required)
**Multiple Size Categories Available:**

**Clothing Sizes:**
- XS, S, M, L, XL, XXL, 3XL, 4XL, 5XL
- Check all sizes you offer
- Multiple selections allowed

**Shoe Sizes (US):**
- 6, 6.5, 7, 7.5, 8, 8.5, 9, 9.5, 10, 10.5, 11, 12, 13
- Half sizes available
- Select all available sizes

**Shoe Sizes (EU):**
- 39, 40, 41, 42, 43, 44, 45, 46, 47
- European sizing standard
- Can select both US and EU

**Pants Sizes (Waist × Length):**
- 26×28, 27×30, 28×30, 29×30, 30×30, 31×30, 32×30, 33×30, 34×30, 36×30, 38×30, 40×30, 42×32, 44×32
- Standard waist and length combinations
- Select all available sizes

**One Size / Free Size:**
- One Size
- Free Size
- For products that fit all sizes

**Custom Sizes:**
- Enter custom size values
- Type size in input field (e.g., "28W", "Medium-Tall", "42R")
- Click "Add Size" button
- Custom size appears as a badge
- Click × to remove custom size
- Useful for unique sizing systems

**Selected Sizes Display:**
- Shows all selected sizes below checkboxes
- Updates in real-time as you select
- Format: "S, M, L, XL, Custom Size"

**Important:**
- At least one size must be selected
- Sizes create product variants with colors
- Each size-color combination tracks stock separately

#### 5. Stock Quantity (Required)
- Enter total available units
- Whole numbers only (no decimals)
- Minimum: 0 (but not recommended)
- Updates automatically when orders are placed
- Distributed across variants if multiple colors/sizes

#### 6. Low Stock Threshold
- Alert trigger level
- Default: 5 units
- Optional but recommended
- You'll receive notifications when stock falls below this level
- Helps prevent stockouts
- Can be different per product

#### 7. Price (Required)
- Enter selling price in Philippine Peso (₱)
- Format: 0.00 (two decimal places)
- Minimum: ₱0.01
- This is the price buyers will pay
- Consider:
  - Platform commission (5%)
  - Shipping costs
  - Competitor pricing
  - Your profit margin

#### 8. Product Color Options (Required)
**Two Options:**

**Option 1: Product has different colors/variations**
- Select this if your product comes in multiple colors
- You'll need to upload separate images for each color
- Each image will be assigned a color name
- Creates color variants (e.g., "Red", "Blue", "Black")
- Buyers can select color when ordering

**Option 2: Product has no color variations**
- Select this if product is one color only
- Or if color doesn't matter (e.g., accessories)
- All images represent the same product
- Creates single "Standard" variant
- Simpler inventory management

**Important:**
- This choice affects how images are handled
- Cannot be easily changed after creation
- Choose carefully based on your product

#### 9. Product Images (Required)
**Upload Area Features:**
- Drag and drop images
- Or click "Choose Files" to browse
- Multiple images supported (unlimited)
- Each image maximum 10MB
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF

**Image Requirements:**
- Minimum: 1 image required
- Recommended: 3-5 images showing different angles
- High resolution recommended (at least 800×800px)
- Clear, well-lit photos
- White or neutral background preferred

**Upload Process:**
1. Click upload area or drag images
2. Images preview immediately
3. Each image shows:
   - Thumbnail preview
   - File name
   - File size
   - Remove button (trash icon)

**If "Has Colors" Selected:**
- Each image gets a color name input field
- Enter color name for each image (e.g., "Red", "Navy Blue", "Forest Green")
- Color names become product variants
- Required for each image
- Example: Image 1 = "Black", Image 2 = "White", Image 3 = "Gray"

**If "No Colors" Selected:**
- No color name inputs appear
- All images represent the same product
- Used for different angles/views
- System creates single "Standard" variant

**Image Management:**
- Remove individual images (trash icon)
- Add more images anytime
- Rearrange by removing and re-uploading
- First image becomes main product image

**Image Preview Display:**
- 3-column grid layout
- Each image in its own card
- Shows image, filename, size
- Color input field (if applicable)
- Remove button on hover

### Form Validation

**Before Submitting, System Checks:**
- Product name is filled
- Description is provided
- Category is selected
- At least one size is selected
- Stock quantity is entered
- Price is valid (greater than 0)
- Color option is selected
- At least one image is uploaded
- If "has colors": all images have color names
- All required fields are complete

**Validation Errors:**
- Red border appears on invalid fields
- Error message displays
- Form won't submit until fixed
- Scroll to first error automatically

### Submitting Your Product

#### Final Steps:
1. Review all information carefully
2. Check image quality and color names
3. Verify pricing and stock
4. Ensure all sizes are selected
5. Click "Add Product" button (green button at bottom)

#### After Submission:
- Processing message appears
- Product is created in database
- Variants are generated automatically:
  - If has colors: Size × Color combinations
  - If no colors: Size × Standard combinations
- Success message displays
- Redirected to Products page
- New product appears in your catalog

#### What Happens Behind the Scenes:
- Product record created
- Images uploaded to server
- Variants created for each size-color combination
- Initial stock distributed (if applicable)
- Product set to Active status
- Available for buyers immediately

### Understanding Variants

**Variants are automatically created based on:**
- Selected sizes
- Color options (from images or "Standard")

**Example 1 - Has Colors:**
- Sizes: S, M, L
- Colors: Red, Blue (from 2 images)
- Variants created: S-Red, S-Blue, M-Red, M-Blue, L-Red, L-Blue (6 variants)

**Example 2 - No Colors:**
- Sizes: S, M, L
- Colors: Standard (automatic)
- Variants created: S-Standard, M-Standard, L-Standard (3 variants)

**Variant Stock:**
- Total stock is distributed across variants
- Manage individual variant stock in Variant Inventory page
- Each variant tracks separately

### Button Options

**Add Product Button:**
- Green button with plus icon
- Submits the form
- Creates product and variants
- Shows loading state while processing

**Cancel Button:**
- Gray button with X icon
- Returns to Products page
- Discards all entered information
- Confirmation prompt appears

### Best Practices

#### Product Information:
- Use clear, descriptive names
- Write detailed descriptions
- Include measurements and specifications
- Mention materials and care instructions
- Be honest about product condition

#### Images:
- Use high-quality photos
- Show multiple angles
- Include close-ups of details
- Use consistent lighting
- White background preferred
- Show product in use (if applicable)

#### Pricing:
- Research competitor prices
- Factor in all costs
- Consider platform commission (5%)
- Price competitively
- Round to .00 or .99 for psychology

#### Sizes and Colors:
- Offer all available sizes
- Use standard size names
- Be specific with color names
- Match color names to actual product
- Use custom sizes when needed

#### Stock Management:
- Set realistic stock quantities
- Configure low stock threshold
- Plan for reorder time
- Don't overcommit inventory

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Uploading low-quality images
- Vague product descriptions
- Incorrect category selection
- Missing size options
- Unrealistic pricing
- Forgetting color names for images
- Not setting low stock threshold
- Incomplete product information

### After Adding Product
1. Product appears in your Products list
2. Visible to buyers immediately
3. Searchable in store
4. Appears in category listings
5. Ready to receive orders

### Next Steps
- Go to Variant Inventory to set stock per variant
- Monitor product performance in Dashboard
- Update product information as needed
- Promote your new product
- Track sales and adjust pricing

## Variant Inventory Management

Manage stock levels for each color and size combination of your products. The Variant Inventory page allows you to update stock quantities for all product variants in one place.

### Accessing Variant Inventory

#### Navigation
1. Go to "Products" page
2. Click "Variant Inventory" button (gray button, top right)
3. Or navigate directly from the menu

### Page Layout

#### Header Section
- **Title**: "Variant Inventory Management"
- **Description**: "Manage stock levels for each color and size combination of your products"
- **Icon**: Boxes icon

#### Filters Section
**Four Filter Options:**

1. **Search Product**
   - Search by product name
   - Real-time filtering as you type
   - Icon: Search (magnifying glass)

2. **Category Filter**
   - All Categories (default)
   - SUITS, BLAZERS, SHIRTS, PANTS, OUTERWEAR, JACKETS, ACTIVEWEAR, SHOES, GROOMING

3. **Stock Status Filter**
   - All Stock Levels (default)
   - Low Stock
   - Out of Stock
   - In Stock

4. **Sort By**
   - Newest First (default)
   - Oldest First
   - Stock: High to Low
   - Stock: Low to High

### Product List Table

#### Table Columns
1. **No.**: Sequential number
2. **Product Name**: Full product name
3. **Category**: Product category badge
4. **Total Stock**: Sum of all variant stock with color-coded indicator
5. **Actions**: "Manage Variants" button

#### Stock Status Indicators
- **Green with checkmark**: Stock is good
- **Orange with warning**: Low stock
- **Red with X**: Out of stock

### Variant Management Modal

#### Opening the Modal
1. Click "Manage Variants" button on any product
2. Modal opens showing all size-color combinations

#### Bulk Update Section
**Three Bulk Update Options:**

1. **Apply to All** - Sets stock for ALL variants
2. **Apply by Color** - Sets stock for all sizes of selected color
3. **Apply by Size** - Sets stock for all colors of selected size

#### Variants Table
**Columns:**
- Color
- Size
- Stock Quantity (editable)
- Status (color-coded badge)

**Status Badges:**
- High (Green): Stock > threshold × 2
- Medium (Blue): Stock > threshold but ≤ threshold × 2
- Low (Orange): Stock > 0 but ≤ threshold
- Out (Red): Stock = 0

### Best Practices
- Update stock regularly
- Use bulk updates for efficiency
- Monitor low stock warnings
- Keep accurate counts

   - Prevents abuse
   - Example: 1 use per customer

3. **Unlimited**
   - No usage restrictions
   - Use with caution

#### Step 6: Conditions
**Minimum Requirements:**
1. **Minimum Purchase Amount**
   - Order must exceed amount
   - Example: ₱500 minimum

2. **Minimum Quantity**
   - Number of items required
   - Example: Buy 2 or more

3. **Specific Products**
   - Apply to selected products only
   - Exclude certain items
